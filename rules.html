Pool Rules
01
$10 per entry — enter as many times as you want. E-transfer to matt.hope@rocketmail.com with your full real name in the memo.
02
All entries must be received before puck drop on opening night. Deadline: September 29, 2026.
03
Select one (1) player per box — 24 boxes total covering forwards, defensemen, and goalies.
04
Points from all 24 players are combined into one total score per entry.
05
No trades. Picks lock at puck drop on September 29, 2026 and cannot be changed for any reason after that.
06
Tie-breaker sequence: total points → total goals + assists → total goalie saves. If still tied, the prize is split equally.
🎩
Hat Trick bonus: Any skater scoring 3+ goals in a single game earns +3 bonus points.
