🏒 How It Works
1
Pick one player from each of 24 boxes
Players are grouped into 24 boxes — 12 Forward boxes, 6 Defense boxes, and 6 Goalie boxes — ranked by prior season pool points. You pick exactly one player per box.
2
Any team can pick the same player
There's no exclusivity. Every participant picks from the same boxes independently — multiple teams can have the same player. Boxes are designed to create strategic choice within each tier.
3
Earn points based on your players' stats
Stats are tracked nightly from NHL game data. Points accumulate throughout the regular season. Standings update automatically each morning.
4
Pick a division winner for bonus points
Select one team to lead each of the four NHL divisions. You earn +10 bonus points on any night your picked team currently leads their division.

🔄 Roster Moves
📋
2 moves per entry, per season
Each team gets 2 roster moves total. Use them wisely — rejected moves do not count against your limit.
📦
Same box only
You can only swap to another player within the same box. You cannot move across position groups or box tiers.
✂️
Points split at approval date
Your dropped player's points up to the approval date are yours to keep. Your new player's points count from the approval date forward only.
✅
Commissioner approval required
All moves require commissioner approval. You'll receive an email confirmation once approved. Moves requested after February 1, 2027 will not be processed.
🔑
How to request a move
Use the link and auth token in your original picks confirmation email to access the Roster Move page. Keep that email — it's your key.

📜 General Rules
Submissions One entry per person. Multiple entries allowed but each requires separate $10 payment.
Payment E-transfer to matt.hope@rocketmail.com with your name in the memo. Unpaid entries may be removed.
Picks lock All picks are final at September 29, 2026 at 7pm ET. No changes after submission or after the deadline.
Stats source All stats sourced directly from the NHL API. Standings update nightly. No manual adjustments.
IR players Injured players earn 0 points while on IR. Use a roster move to replace them if eligible.
